About Bicol Gold Capital

The most trusted gold buyer in the Bicol Region, offering discreet and fair transactions for all your gold selling needs.

Our Story

Bicol Gold Capital was established with a simple mission: to provide the people of Bicol Region with a trustworthy, discreet, and fair option for selling their gold.

We understand that selling gold can be a sensitive matter, which is why we prioritize confidentiality and discretion in all our transactions. Whether you're selling family heirlooms, investment gold, or items with papel de ahensya, we offer competitive rates without unnecessary questions.

Our team consists of experienced gold buyers who can accurately appraise gold of any karat and condition, ensuring you get the best value for your items.
